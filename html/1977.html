Slap Shot - (7.2 from 14239 votes)Comedy | Drama | Sport

    IMDB Tagline: Slap Shot out slaps... out swears... out laughs...
    IMDB Comment: My favorite sports movie

Director George Roy Hill (Butch Cassidy and the Sundance Kid) delivers a funny, foul-mouthed sports comedy about former hockey star Reggie Dunlop (Paul Newman), who goes from living the high life to coaching a minor-league hockey team on the skids. When Dunlop realizes that playing dirty excites the crowds, he turns his team into a goon squad of thugs whose popularity only grows the longer they stew in the penalty box.
Suspiria - (7.4 from 16468 votes)Horror | Mystery

    IMDB Tagline: The Only Thing More Terrifying Than The Last 12 Minutes Of This Film Are The First 92.
    IMDB Comment: The Masterpiece of Dario Argento

This horror gem follows Susan (Jessica Harper), a nave young American girl whose talents have brought her to an illustrious European ballet school. But once she gets there, she realizes there's something strange going on as she's faced with a cluster of freaky happenings, from a shower of maggots to poison in her food. What she soon learns is that the school has been a meeting place for witches for many years.
The Duellists - (7.4 from 6005 votes)Action | Adventure | Drama | War

    IMDB Tagline: Fencing is a science. Loving is a passion. Duelling is an obsession.
    IMDB Comment: definitely worth watching

D'Hubert (Keith Carradine) and Feraud (Harvey Keitel) are officers in Napoleon's army who challenge each other to a series of bloody duels. In the final clash -- which comes after many years of fighting -- D'Hubert finally bests Feraud, but things don't end there. Better keep your powder dry for the explosive finish! Based on a story by Joseph Conrad, The Duellists marks director Ridley Scott's feature debut.
The Turning Point - (7.0 from 1484 votes)Drama | Romance

    IMDB Tagline: The generations change. But the choices remain the same.
    IMDB Comment: Timeless coming of age

Herbert Ross's classic drama from the 1970s stars Shirley MacLaine as DeeDee Rodgers, a former classical ballerina who cut short her rise to fame when she decided instead to settle down and raise children. But when her daughter, Emilia (Leslie Browne), shows promise as a ballerina, DeeDee is forced to confront the choices she made in the past and face off with Emma (Anne Bancroft), a famous dancer who longs for what DeeDee has.
Oh, God! - (6.3 from 3818 votes)Comedy | Fantasy

    IMDB Tagline: Its true. People have trouble remembering My Words. Moses had such a bad memory I had to give him tablets.
    IMDB Comment: Simple and warm.

Buttonholing venerable comedian George Burns for the title role was certainly a divine inspiration! God, in the guise of a wisecracking old gent, decides to makes his presence known to a harried grocery clerk (John Denver) so he can get the message out that "everything on earth can still work if we want it to." Filled with pithy one-liners delivered by the master, Oh, God! was scripted by "M*A*S*H" creator Larry Gelbart.
