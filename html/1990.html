Rosencrantz Guildenstern Are Dead - (7.5 from 9946 votes)Comedy | Drama

    IMDB Tagline:
    IMDB Comment: The Play Without the Play

Director Tom Stoppard turns William Shakespeare's tragedy "Hamlet" topsy-turvy in this witty, existential puzzle box, presenting the play from the view of trivial characters Rosencrantz (Gary Oldman) and Guildenstern (Tim Roth). Beckoned to find the cause of the Danish prince's malaise, the duo strolls into the midst of the royal intrigue. But with no memory of Hamlet (or anything else), they're mystified by their mission.
Avalon - (7.2 from 3141 votes)Drama

    IMDB Tagline: Three generations of family. They shared a dream called America in a place called Avalon.
    IMDB Comment: Wonderful walk down memory lane

Based on director Barry Levinson's memories of life with his immigrant grandparents, this nostalgic story (which represents the final installment in Levinson's "Baltimore Trilogy") was nominated for four Oscars. Sam Krichinsky (Armin Mueller-Stahl) comes to America in 1914 and marries Eva (Joan Plowright); years later, this multigenerational Jewish family faces a culture clash amidst its own personal struggles and triumphs.

    IMDB Tagline: A love that will last forever.
    IMDB Comment: This is one of the most touching love stories ever made.

Barry Gifford's neopulp novel inspired this controversial cult film from director David Lynch.
